AI Diagnostic Deployment

Enhancing Arrhythmia Diagnosis

A multi-modal framework deploying deep learning for ECG classification and Augmented Reality for 3D heart visualization.

Clinical Methodology

01. Signal Acquisition & Preprocessing

High-fidelity ECG data is cleaned and normalized using digital filters to ensure accurate neural network ingestion.

02. Deep Learning Classification

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) classify rhythms into specific arrhythmia types (AFib, PVC, Tachycardia) with real-time accuracy.

03. AR Spatial Visualization

The classified results are projected onto a 3D Heart model, enabling clinicians to interact with the anatomical region affected.

Interactive 3D Engine

Deploying via ARCore/Unity for spatial surgical planning.
