📚 ECG Theory

ECG components:

  • P wave — atrial depolarization
  • PR interval — AV conduction
  • QRS — ventricular depolarization
  • ST & T — repolarization

Interpretation:

  1. Check rate & rhythm
  2. Measure PR & QRS
  3. Look for abnormalities
