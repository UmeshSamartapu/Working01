Arrhythmia Diagnosis Deployment

ECG Deep Learning Classification & AR 3D Interaction Interface

Project Overview

This project introduces a cutting-edge clinical framework designed to enhance arrhythmia diagnosis through the synergy of Artificial Intelligence and Augmented Reality (AR). By processing ECG datasets through high-accuracy Deep Learning models, the system classifies cardiac rhythms in real-time.

The core innovation lies in the deployment of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for visual signal analysis and Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) for structured patient data. These outputs are integrated into an interactive AR environment, where medical professionals can visualize and interact with a 3D heart model that highlights pathological electrical pathways based on the AI's classification results. This "human-in-the-loop" approach ensures that machine precision is balanced with clinical expertise, optimizing diagnostic reliability for modern cardiovascular healthcare.
